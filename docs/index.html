Auto Sync

Smart SMS Parsing — Every Shilling Captured

Pesalytics automatically reads every M-PESA alert — Buy Goods, PayBill, Send Money, Withdrawals, Received, Pochi la Biashara, Airtime, and Fuliza overdrafts. A two-tier regex engine maps each message to the right transaction type, then your own custom rules train it further.

Parses all 8+ M-PESA transaction types automatically
Detects and separates carrier fee transactions
Incremental sync — after first import, only new messages are scanned
Custom rules: remap any payee to a category, applied retroactively

Privacy First

Your data never leaves your phone. Ever.

Pesalytics was built with a non-negotiable premise: M-PESA data is deeply personal. Every computation — parsing, categorizing, analyzing — runs locally on your hardware. The INTERNET permission exists solely for Google Play Billing. That's it.
